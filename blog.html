Cashew Benefits

1. They are also rich in calcium and promote bone health.

The magnesium in cashews is also important for bone formation as it helps with the assimilation of calcium into the bone. Manganese, another mineral in cashews, has been shown to prevent osteoporosis in combination with calcium and copper.

2. Eating cashew nuts has often been related to cancer prevention.

Cashew nuts contain proanthocyanidins, a class of flavanols that actually starve tumors and prevent cancer cells from dividing. In some studies, it’s been shown that eating 12 cashews a day can reduce your colon cancer risk.

3. The fibre in them helps in better digestion.

Cashews contain oleic acid and palmitic acid, which are not produced by our bodies. Therefore, by consuming this nut, you can gain much of these fibers, which are good for digestion.
