Commitment to the Environment

Sustainable and Responsible Procurement

We maintain high ethical standards with our suppliers, including smallholder farmers and farm-gate aggregators. Raw cashew nuts are predominately grown in economically fragile areas.

Sustainable and Responsible Processing

Keeping our people safe is our first and primary responsibility as a company. Safety at work is a fundamental human right, and workplace conditions directly influence workers as well as their families.

Sustainable and Responsible Storage & Transport

Storage and Transport plays a vital role in the cashew value chain and is critical for value optimisation. Our energy- efficient storage facilities across origins and processing destinations have been built and maintained sustainably and responsibly.

Sustainable and Responsible Distribution

We are committed to creating a sustainable and responsible supply chain that helps us to distribute our cashew nuts worldwide in a way that respects the environment.
